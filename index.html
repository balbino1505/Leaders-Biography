Biography of East Timor Guerrilla and Politicians Leaders

Xanana Gusmão

José Alexandre "Xanana" Gusmão GColIH GCL GCMG is an East Timorese politician. A former rebel, he was the first President of the independent East Timor, serving from 2002 to 2007.

Ramos Horta

José Manuel Ramos-Horta GColIH GCL is an East Timorese politician currently serving as president of East Timor since May 2022. He previously served as president from 20 May 2007 to 20 May 2012. Previously he was Minister of Foreign Affairs from 2002 to 2006 and Prime Minister from 2006 to 2007.

Taur Matan Ruak

José Maria Vasconcelos GColIH, popularly known as Taur Matan Ruak, is an East Timorese politician who has served as the prime minister of East Timor since 22 June 2018. He was also President of East Timor from 20 May 2012 to 20 May 2017

Francisco Guterres "Lú-Olo"

Francisco Guterres, popularly known as Lú-Olo, is an East Timorese politician who served as president of East Timor from 20 May 2017 to 20 May 2022.

Lere Anan Timur

Major General Lere Anan Timur is a Timorese general who has served as Commander of the F-FDTL since 6 October 2011.
